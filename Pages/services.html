Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes—veggie burgers, chips, salads, and custom bowls. Ask for meat-free gravy and separate prep.

Are your products Halaal-friendly?

We source from Halaal-certified suppliers where possible and avoid cross-contamination. Please ask if you have specific requirements.

Can I pre-order for a specific time?

Yes. Place your order at least 30 minutes in advance and tell us your collection time.
